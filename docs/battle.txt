ELEMENTS OF A BATTLE

2 KINDS:
- wild
- trainer

BOTH INCLUDE:
- bag
    - healing, reviving, status healing, battle items, throwing balls [BLOCKED BY TRAINER]
- switch
- attack

ONLY WILD INCLUDES:
- run
- catching pokemon


What can happen inside a battle?
-someone wins/loses
-a pokemon is caught
-normal battle things [bag, switch, attack]
